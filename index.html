About Us

Popstar Productions was founded by husband and wife duo, Danny and Danea Sullivan. With Danny's experience as a professional baseball player and Danea's history as a Disney World dancer, they both understand the intricate details and dedication necessary for success. Their diverse experiences are the backbone of their commitment to taking your event to a higher level.
