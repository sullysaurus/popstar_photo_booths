Our Booths

Camera System

Our Booths offer the most versatile camera system and highest quality on the market. Print 24 megapixel photos and capture groups of any size with studio quality lighting. Our printer is super fast and wireless so guests receive high quality photos immediately.

Sharing

Photos are instantly uploaded to your personal gallery where guests can view and share in real time. Our system offers text, email and bluetooth photo sharing (if there is no internet). Chose from strip style 2x6 prints, classic 4x6 prints, perforated media, sticker media, or even panoramic!

Features

Squares, prints, video confessionals, wide shots, horizontal shots, boomerangs, filters, gifs, black and white, overlays are just a few of the features your guests will have at just a screen tap away.
